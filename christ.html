MEN ON MISSION

Men on Mission is an interdenominational Christian platform instituted to serve the body of Christ by addressing fatherhood and by extension the family. Man is the foundation of God creation. No father no nation, the quality of fathers determine the quality of a nation. This is the burden of Men on Mission. It was commissioned in 2016 and since increase in impact. We run seminars, training for fathers and couples, we counsels and mentor the future fathers.

METHODS

Biblical Teachings
Training and Seminar
Modelling, Mentoring
counselling
Prayers

OUR VISION

To raise strong, skilful and purposeful fathers who will father their homes in Godliness.

OUR MISSION

To see Godly fathers leading Godly homes raising Godly children to champion Godly society to the
glory of God.

BOARD OF TRUSTEE

ENGR. ISAAC OMOSULE NENUWA

is the chairman board of trustee of Men on Mission. He is raised from the Redeemed
Christian Church of God. He is a pastor, father educator, speaker and a writer. He
speaks to youths, Fathers and families. He had served in the leadership capacity of
various ministries across the Nation. He is the National Administrative secretary for
Fellowship of Christian Traditional Rulers of Nigeria (FECTRON) He is a licence preacher
and widely travels. He is passionate to see the emergence of Godly families across the
nation. He holds a master degree in Mechanical Engineering, a PGD in theology and
Alumina of Institute of National Transformation .He is a lecturer at Rufus Giwa
Polytechnics, Owo, Ondo state Nigeria. He is married and blessed with children.

MATTHEW OLAYINKA OLADIPO (an entrepreneur by profession)

is the secretary of Men on Mission Board of Trustee. He was the pioneer
President/Coordinator of Redeemed Christian Corpers Fellowship (RCCF) at Ondo West
Local Government of Ondo State between 2012-2013.He is the current Youth President
of RCCG Mercy Seat Zone at Ondo Province Four (4). He is also the Managing Director of
Matmos International Limited. He is a motivational speaker. He holds M.sc in Mass
Communication, PGD in Journalism, PGD in Customer Relationship Management, and
Higher Diploma certificate in Mass Communication. He is also an associate member of
Nigerian Institute of Public Relations (ANIPR). He is happily married and blessed with
children.

OTUNLA SAMUEL OLU

a professional Accountant with the experience spans over 15 years is the Vice chairman

Church of with over 19 years experience as a Minister. He had served at various
Parishes as a Minister and currently Parish Pastor at City of Peace Parish in kajola,

OJO OLUMUYIWA CURTIS
